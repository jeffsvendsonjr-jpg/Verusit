# Privacy Policy for VerusIT

**Effective Date:** January 2025

## Overview
VerusIT provides metadata analysis for Google Search results. We prioritize user privacy and data minimization.

## Data Collection & Usage
**VerusIT does not collect, transmit, or store user data.**
* **No Remote Processing:** All analysis (date extraction, pattern matching) happens locally on your device.
* **No Analytics:** We do not use third-party tracking software.
* **No Search History:** We do not log or save your search queries.
